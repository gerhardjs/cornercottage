Discover a Beachside Getaway

Corner Cottage offers comfortable accommodation just steps away from the beautiful Melkbosstrand Beach. Located in Cape Town, this apartment is the perfect retreat for beach lovers and those seeking relaxation. The air-conditioned apartment features a cozy bedroom, a spacious living room, and a fully equipped kitchen with a fridge and a coffee machine. The bathroom is equipped with a shower and a hairdryer. Towels and bed linen are provided for your convenience. Free private parking and complimentary WiFi are available for guests. Stay connected and explore the nearby attractions hassle-free. CTICC (Cape Town International Convention Centre) is within easy reach, located just 29 km away. Don't miss the opportunity to visit Robben Island, as the ferry terminal is only 31 km from the property. The apartment is approximately 40 km from Cape Town International Airport.

  • Only 300 metres from Melkbosstrand Beach
  • Fully equipped kitchen with a fridge and a coffee machine
  • Free private parking and complimentary WiFi

Immerse yourself in a world of relaxation and tranquility at Corner Cottage. Enjoy the breathtaking views, take leisurely strolls along the beach, and indulge in the serenity of this beachside getaway.
